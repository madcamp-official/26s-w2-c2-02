# Grill Me

Interrogate the plan until the weak spots are visible.

## What to probe

- hidden assumptions
- edge cases
- rollback strategy
- operational constraints
- missing tests
- vague ownership
- scope creep

## Style

- Ask pointed, concrete questions.
- Prefer failure scenarios over abstract criticism.
- Keep pushing until the proposal can survive implementation pressure.

## End state

Finish with:

- the sharpest risks
- what needs clarification
- what now looks solid
